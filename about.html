About Us

Training isn't a race, but a marathon.

Here at Daniel's Gym, we are dedicated to have people push past their limits and go further beyond. Whether it's via bodybuilding, powerlifting, calisthenics, boxing, MMA or even basic exercises, the path is open to you!

Full Mind, Full Body, Full Power!

Having a good amount of rest is very important! There'll be no gains earned if you're too tired! No need to torture yourself; even 4-5 days of working out is more than enough!

Form Over Weight!

The amount of weight doesn't matter if proper form isn't achieved. If your form is good, your gains are secured! Pretty soon, your strength will go all the way up!

Focus on Your Diet

Diet is key! To get your washboard abs, Death-Star delts or a D-cup-sized chest, you need to eat proper! Get your protein, get your healthy fats, get your carbs (if needed) and transform your body!

Never Too Late!

Young, old, sick, disabled and all of the above, it's never too late to get your workout in! You'll be surprised who is supporting you on your journey! Even the best of the best had to start somewhere, and it wasn't at the best point! So get your game on!
